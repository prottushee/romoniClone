About

Romoni is the first and currently the largest salon-at-home brand in Bangladesh. Founded in 2018, Romoni provides a 360 platform for women micro-entrepreneurs in the beauty industry to start, run and grow their salon-at-home business. Romoni standardizes and facilitates the entire process by providing skills training.

Why Romoni?

Experienced & Verified Experts

At Romoni, we prioritize professionalism and excellence. Our clients are served by highly experienced and verified beauticians to ensure the best possible experience.

Branded & Sealed Products

We use only premium, branded products for all our services. Our commitment to quality ensures that you receive the best treatments right at your home.

How it Works

  • Place Your Order

    Select your desired beauty service through our website or app.

  • Verification

    Our system confirms your order, ensuring services are tailored for women.

  • Matching Expertise

    We match you with the best local beautician for your chosen service.

  • Beautician Confirmation

    The beautician accepts and prepares the order via the Romoni app.

  • Pre-Visit Call

    The beautician will contact you before the appointment to confirm the schedule.

  • Arrival

    Your beautician arrives at your home at the agreed time.

  • Safety First

    Ensure a secure environment when welcoming your beautician inside.

  • Service Delivery

    Relax and enjoy high-quality beauty services delivered with care.
